# Attribution & Data Licenses

## Audio clips — Google FLEURS (CC-BY-4.0)

The speech audio clips bundled in this repository (under
`unity/Assets/StreamingAssets/clips/`, and produced by the pipeline in `prep/`)
are derived from the **FLEURS** dataset:

> **FLEURS: Few-shot Learning Evaluation of Universal Representations of Speech.**
> Google Research. https://huggingface.co/datasets/google/fleurs

FLEURS is the speech extension of the **FLoRes** machine-translation benchmark
(the underlying sentence text corpus):

> **The FLoRes-101 / FLoRes-200 Evaluation Benchmark.** Meta AI / No Language
> Left Behind. https://github.com/facebookresearch/flores

Both are distributed under the **Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International
(CC-BY-4.0)** license: https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/

Clips are redistributed here under CC-BY-4.0 with attribution as required. This
project makes no claim of ownership over the FLEURS/FLoRes data. The in-game
"Data & Licenses" credits screen (brief §17) will carry this same attribution
before any public release of the game.
